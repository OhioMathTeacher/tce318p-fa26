The principle
A good Before warms up the same mathematics your task investigates and surfaces the learner's entry thinking — but it withholds the task's move, so the task can still reveal what students bring.
The test to carry
After your routine, could a student still show you something genuinely their own on the task —
or did the routine already do the task's work?
